The Zen Room

One floor · Three purposes · Sleep · Work · Meditation
The room changes shape four times a day.
Whichever mode is ending, leave it ready for whichever mode is next.

The Six Objects

Each ritual object · One handling rule
— THE GOVERNING RULE —
Touch each object only the way it asks to be touched. If you don't know — leave it.
⛯
Mabkhar
Charcoal lit with tongs until grey-white, then one chip of oud or bukhoor.
❦
Oud · Frankincense
Fingernail-sized piece per evening. One chip burns fully before adding a second.
⌇
Shakuhachi
Blow across, not into, the angled mouthpiece. Wipe inside dry. Rests on its silk sleeve.
◉
Astrolabe
Lift by the mater only — never the rete. Soft cloth only. No polish.
☉
Orrery
Turn slowly in the engraved arrow's direction only. One revolution ≈ one Earth-year.
◯
Rin Bell
Strike on the side with the wooden mallet, never the rim. Three slow strikes — for transitions only.
