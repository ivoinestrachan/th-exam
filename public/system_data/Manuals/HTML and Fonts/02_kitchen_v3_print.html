— The Golden Rule —

When you use a dish, put it in the dishwasher.
If the dishwasher is full or running, wash it by hand.

No third option. No "later." No sink-stacking.
ii. — Daily Rituals

When You…

cook

  • Wipe down the steel counters.
  • Clean the stove top.
  • Clear the sink completely.
  • Return all condiments and sauces to their shelves.
  • Clean every pot you used.
  • Gather and clean all plates and utensils.
  • Spray the air freshener before you leave.

get yourself a drink

  • Rinse the glass and place it on the drying rack straight away.

make coffee

  • Throw out the used coffee grinds.
  • Clean the milk frother.
