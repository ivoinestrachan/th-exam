— THE RULE —
Every cable, pen and device returns to its assigned shelf, immediately after use.
Always
  • Coil chargers before storing
  • Cap pens and markers
  • Box old laptops
  • Stack notebooks flat
Never
  • Leave cables tangled
  • Stack weight on iPads
  • Mix stationery with devices
  • Store damaged batteries
If cables are tangled → untangle, coil, return. If a battery is damaged or leaking → remove, isolate, dispose at recycling. If something heavy is on an iPad → remove, inspect screen.
— REMEMBER —
Chargers, stationery, notebooks, laptops — each on their own shelf
